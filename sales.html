Now also available, exclusive art prints.

Most images by Bert Schaeffer shown on this website, are available for purchase in the form of exclusive Art Prints. They can serve as unique home decoration or as a statement piece for your professional environment.

  • Every image is available in limited edition only.
  • Every single piece is signed by the photographer.
  • The artprints will be accompanied by a certificate of authenticity.
  • Standard sizes are 100cm x 150cm and 130cm x 200cm.
  • Images are printed on ARTEX, white DIBOND and LIQUID GLOSS or other materials.

ARTEX is a pvc/polyether material with a very subtle texture. It is stretched on an aluminium frame. DIBOND is a light weight and stiff aluminium/polyethylene laminate.

LIQUID GLOSS is a two-component epoxy resin system designed to provide a tough, durable and flexible high-gloss coating to photographs. LIQUID GLOSS has a natural augmenting and illuminating quality, making colors and lines stand out.

  • Both ARTEX and DIBOND:
    100cm x 150cm E1500,- | 130cm x 200cm E2300,-

  • Both LIQUID GLOSS and DIBOND:
    80cm x 120cm E1950,- | 120cm x 180cm E2950,-

  • Prices for the Church series have a different price.
  • Other sizes and different materials are available on demand.
